Surface Types

We Clean Every Surface

Different surfaces demand different techniques. We know them all.

Concrete

The most common driveway type in Adelaide. High-pressure washing removes deep-set stains and restores colour uniformity.

Pavers

Brick and stone pavers require careful pressure control to clean without dislodging jointing sand. We handle this precisely.

Exposed Aggregate

Sensitive to abrasive pressure. We use the right technique to clean the surface while preserving the aggregate texture.

Asphalt

Asphalt is cleaned with lower-pressure, controlled washing to remove surface grime without damaging the bitumen binding.
