Our Process

How It Works

A structured, safe approach designed to deliver maximum results with minimal disruption.

01

Site Assessment & Quote

We assess your facility to understand its size, layout, usage, and specific requirements. We then provide a tailored, no-obligation quote with a recommended cleaning schedule.

02

Schedule & Access Planning

We coordinate with your operations team to schedule cleaning during planned downtime, maintenance windows or off-peak hours — minimising impact on your business.

Preparation & Equipment Setup

Our team arrives with all required equipment and cleaning agents. Where needed, forklift-licensed personnel assist with moving stock or equipment to ensure safe and thorough access.

03
04

Full Freezer Clean

We systematically clean floors, walls, ceilings, shelving, doors, seals and high-contact surfaces — removing ice build-up, dirt, debris, mould and contaminants throughout the entire space.

Final Inspection & Handover

We conduct a final walkthrough to ensure all areas meet the required standard, restore any moved equipment and provide a completion report including photos for your records.
