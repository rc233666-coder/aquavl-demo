Our Process

How It Works

A structured approach to ongoing commercial property maintenance that delivers consistent results.

01

Site Consultation & Audit

We visit your property to conduct a thorough assessment — identifying all exterior areas, surfaces, access requirements and current condition. This forms the basis of your tailored maintenance proposal.

02

Tailored Program Proposal

We present a clear, itemised maintenance program detailing services, frequencies, scheduling and pricing. Everything is transparent — no surprises, no hidden costs.

Scheduled Service Delivery

Our team delivers each service to a consistently high standard, working within agreed time windows to minimise disruption to your business, tenants or operations.

03
04

Completion Reports

After every service visit, we provide a completion report with photos documenting the work carried out. This ensures full transparency and provides a record for strata managers and property owners.

Ongoing Review & Communication

We maintain open communication with your team, flagging any maintenance concerns we identify during our visits and adjusting your program as your property's needs evolve over time.
